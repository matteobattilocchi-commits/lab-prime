Services

Where we add value.

We focus on the operational middle: the space between scientific teams, estates, vendors, and leadership where projects either become controlled or become painful.

01

Lab and facility operations

We support day-to-day operational control in research and biotech settings where reliability matters.

  • Operational reviews and issue mapping.
  • Cold storage and equipment uptime planning.
  • Vendor, service, and maintenance coordination.
  • Incident follow-up and corrective action structure.

02

Projects and technical readiness

We provide structured support for changes that cut across labs, estates, facilities, and users.

  • Lab relocations, refurbishments, and decant planning.
  • Equipment installation readiness and utilities coordination.
  • Stakeholder mapping, governance, and delivery tracking.
  • Risk registers and practical mitigation plans.

03

Science infrastructure advisory

We advise where research environments need both technical understanding and operational realism.

  • Lab workflow and space-use assessment.
  • BMS and facilities interface translation for scientific users.
  • Procurement support for specialist equipment and services.
  • Operational handover planning for new or refurbished areas.

04

Interim leadership support

We provide short-term cover where an organisation needs experienced operators to steady delivery.

  • Temporary lab operations management.
  • Cross-team coordination during transition periods.
  • Priority setting for overloaded teams.
  • Clear reporting for senior stakeholders.

05

Installation support for science technology providers

We act as the on-the-ground project partner for science technology companies deploying into research institutions.

  • Client-side project management for system installations at research sites.
  • Coordination across facilities, science, H&S, and procurement teams.
  • Acceptance testing, user onboarding, and post-installation integration.
  • A single experienced point of contact who speaks both technology and lab operations.
